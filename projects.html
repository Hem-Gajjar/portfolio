MY PROJECTS

Technostuff Sales and Inventory System
The year 2021 saw the commencement of an exhilarating expedition into the domain of VB.NET development—a voyage that would redefine my professional trajectory. From that initial step, my focus was resolute, leaving behind any contemplation of retreat. Yet, it was a specific juncture during my fifth diploma semester that would emerge as a defining cornerstone along this journey. Amidst my academic pursuits, I undertook the formidable challenge of crafting the "Technostuff Sales and Inventory System." This project, which converged programming finesse with business insight, aimed to revolutionize sales and inventory management. Rooted in VB.NET, it aspired to streamline the intricate frameworks underpinning modern business operations.


Planet Shopping Cart System
In 2022, I began my ASP.NET development journey and took on an exciting project during my 6th semester of diploma studies – the "Planet Shopping Cart System." This e-commerce website, complete with payment gateway and privileges features, ignited my passion for web development. Overcoming challenges and honing my skills, I now strive to create impactful solutions that enrich the online experience for users and leave a lasting mark in the world of ASP.NET development.
